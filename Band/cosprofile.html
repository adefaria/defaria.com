Band Profile

Cast of Shadows was formed in or around October, 1991 and, unfortunately, broke up in April, 1994. We did manage to put out a CD called Face the Time of all original compositions. The band consists of 3 members:


Mike Fraser
bass, lead vocals, keysboards

Scott Dinn
drums, percussion, background vocals

Andrew DeFaria
guitar, background vocals

If you want to get in touch with us (we're still alive!), then contact me.
