Mike Fraser

Mike Fraser plays primarily bass in the band (occasionally acoustic guitar) and was our lead vocalist as well as keyboardist. This was made possible by the fact that the band ran a MIDI sequencer to play all the keyboard parts as well as other things like a sampler and even ran the light show on occasion!
