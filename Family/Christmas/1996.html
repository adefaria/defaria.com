Christmas 1996

Merry Christmas everybody! Welcome to my first electronic Christmas letter. Above is a picture of my favorite girl in the world, my 4 1/2 year old daughter Danielle DeFaria, sitting on Santa's lap in the Valley Fair Mall.

This year has been a pretty good one for Danielle and I and I hope it has been good for you and your family too. Not too much to talk about this year as it's the first time I'm doing this, it's close to Christmas and, like most of you, I have to get my Christmas stuff done too. Next year I hope to add more information here. Perhaps I'll automate it a bit so that you can remove yourself from this list if you do not wish to get a Christmas letter from me. Or maybe I'll setup something that will help you setup your own electronic Christmas list.

This Christmas letter is a web page but I don't have a way to put it out on the public Internet yet.
