Baby It's Cold Outside - Brian Setzer

Why this song made the list

Classic song written by Frank Loesser in 1944, Baby It's Cold Outside would never fly in today's politically correct, woke society. Yet still, does anybody think that this doesn't go on?

This is Brian Setzer's version which I really like. Love the interplay between the man and the woman and the real meaning behind the words.
