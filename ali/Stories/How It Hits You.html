How It Hits You - Jon Anderson

Why this song made the list

Jon Anderson, lead singer for the progressive rock act, Yes , for decades and decades, did this Christmas album, Three Ships, a while back and I've always loved it at this time of year.

Basic theme here from the lyrics is "The spirit of love will always connect you" . Notice how it goes into Ding Dong Merrily on High briefly.
