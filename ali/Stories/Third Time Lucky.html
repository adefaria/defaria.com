Third Time Lucky - Basia Trzetrzelewska

Why this song made the list

Another sultry singer, Basia Trzetrzelewska (say that 3 times fast!), Polish by heritage, married a British piano playing bloke and does Brazilian samba music.

It's interesting that Basia here sings about meeting potential lovers but sticking to her guns that she has her career to pursue. There's a line here "He found my joie de vivre so cute and rather touching". I had to look up what joie de vivre meant. When Jackie asked me what I thought of you Ali, I said "I find her joie de vivre so cute and rather touching". I don't think Jackie got the full meaning here.
