Driving a Stick

Danni has been getting into helping Daddy drive. She no longer needs to use a car seat and sits strapped into the front seat. I had brought Mary-Anne's old Dodge Colt and it had a stick shift. Danni became curious and offered to help shift the stick for me. I told her that I was in a parking lot right now and I couldn't let her do it. She said that she would wait until we got into the "free road" and then she would shift it for me.
