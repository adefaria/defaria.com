Horseback Riding

I took Dannielle horseback riding this month. I have gone to these stables before mostly to show Dannielle the horses as she was afraid of them. This time she seemed to like the idea of trying to ride one. Well we tried but she was just to scared of the horse. So we hung around and watched other little kids ride horses.

Eventually Danni wanted to try again. I was leary as she was scared the first time but she managed to get on a pony named Aboo (after the character in Disney's Aladdin movie). After getting accustomed to Aboo she was even saying "Gittiup!". I quickly taught her the other command: Whoa!
