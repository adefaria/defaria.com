Goat goes potty!

We are trying hard to get Danni interested in the "potty". One day I had her at a petting zoo. She was generally interested in the "Oink Oink" (pig) who was sleeping at the time. "Wake up Oink Oink! Wake up Oink Oink!" she said gentlely nudging the pig. She was a little afraid of the goat because of its size. Then I noticed that the goat was ah, relieving himself and thought that it would be a good to show Danni that other animals urinate too. "Look Danni, the goat's going potty" I said and she acknowledged that saying "Goat goes potty?". "Yes", I said, "the goat goes potty too". "Oh", she said, "goat goes potty".

Later that day, when returning Danni to her Mom, we were discussing what we did that day when Danni looked at her Mom, spread her legs apart, bent slightly forward and said "Mommy, goat goes potty!".
