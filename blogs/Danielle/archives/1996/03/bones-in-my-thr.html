Bones in my Throat

  • Posted on March 17, 1996
  • by

When Danni was younger she was having dinner with her Grandparents. They were having chicken and her Grandpa was concerned about Danni choking on the chicken bones. He cautioned her to he careful or she would get bones in her throat. Danni associated having bones in your throat with having a cough. For a long time Danni would say that she had bones in her throat when she really meant that she had a cough.
